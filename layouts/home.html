👋 Welcome!

Rachael Gibson is a Merseyside-based guitarist and composer whose work delves into tactile approaches to music-making, exploring how alternative notation and technology can enhance the interplay between touch and performance. She is pursuing a PhD in Composition at the University of Liverpool, funded by the NWCDTP. Her recent projects include participation in the Britten Pears Young Artist scheme, creating installation work for the Wanderscapes Project at the Old Courts Wigan, and involvement in the Yorkshire Sound Women’s Network Sound Pioneers Residency. Rachael has composed for a range of media, including theatre, short films, and various organizations such as Whatstick Theatre, The Coach House in partnership with Everyman Theatre Liverpool, Twisted Showcase Productions, Claire House Children’s Hospice, and DaDa Fest.
